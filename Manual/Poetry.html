On the Subject of Poetry

Will you write your way into her heart?

  • You will see a member of the Poetry Club on the module. Write a poem for her using her favorite words.
  • To determine the correct word(s) out of the 6 words provided on the module, calculate the distance between the name of the shown girl and each word using the table below.
  • To calculate the distance between two words, start from the cell containing the first word and jump from one cell to a horizontally or vertically (not diagonally) neighboring cell. Count the minimum number of jumps needed to reach to the cell containing the second word.
  • Pick any word with the least distance to advance. After picking 3 words correctly module will be disarmed. Picking a wrong word will result in a strike and the words will reset.
