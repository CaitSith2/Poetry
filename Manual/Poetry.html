Will you write the way into her heart?

  • You will see a member of the Literature Club.
  • You will write a poem for her by picking one of the 6 possible words in several stages.
  • On each stage you must pick the word she will like the most.
  • You can rank how much a word is liked by a girl by ordering the words by the distance(sum of the horizontal and vertical cell count between words [This needs better phrasing I am aware B). I wanted to be clear while testing.]) to her corner on the table.
  • If there are more than one word with the least distance you can pick any.
